What is the Study Bank?

The Study Bank is a collection of student created notes for every subject a tutee might need them for. The purpose of these notes is to help tutees with resources in addition to their one-on-one meetings with their tutors. They will reinforce learning and provide students with extra opportunities for practice and success.

Want Volunteer/CAS Hours?

Do you lack volunteer/CAS hours? Submitting study notes to the FMM Tutor Club's Study BankFMM Tutor Club's Study Bank is the perfect way to earn them! For each page of notes you will receive 30 minutes of volunteer/CAS hours. Make sure to follow these guidelines for submitting notes:
Rules:
1. Make sure notes are legible and clear.
2. Online notes are preferred but handwritten will be accepted as long as it is neat and easy to read.
3. You cannot submit quizs, tests, or past assignments/assessments that were evaluated.
4. Ensure that the Google Docs or PDFs are titled in the following format: (academic or IB_subject_unit)
For example, (academic_mathematics_linear-relations)
5. If you are submitting notes for more than 1 subject, fill out a different Google Form per subject.
6. No inappropriate submissions will be allowed.
